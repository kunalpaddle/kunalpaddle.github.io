Meal Point Exchange



Every year, hundreds of students at Cal try to buy and sell meal points by posting on several Facebook pages, hoping for a response, and then painstakingly arranging a meeting with a buyer/seller in person. Meal Point Exchange for Android solves this problem. Students will be able to "check in" to dining halls based on their GPS location as buyers or sellers, name a price, and pay one another quickly and painlessly.


Doge Generator


In January, I attended my first hackathon without any knowledge of Android, and wanted to learn the basics fast. By the time the hackathon finished, Doge Generator was born. Doge Generator for Android is a simple, intentionally-tacky joke app that produces a "Doge", a popular internet meme. Within a week, the app gained over 1500 downloads.
